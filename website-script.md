## 4. Platform pillars (the 5 C's)

**Eyebrow:** THE PLATFORM

**Headline:** Own the loop, not just the license.

Five cards:

**Control**
Your data remains yours. Your private evals define what "good" looks like for your business. Memory, traces, feedback, and decisions stay inside your boundary — and you keep the right to learn from your own outputs.

**Context**
We build your enterprise context graph: the living map of your workflows, entities, and institutional knowledge that makes any model dramatically better at *your* problems.

**Choice**
The orchestration layer is decoupled from every model. Swap GPT for Claude for Gemini for open weights — your evals, routing, and context graph don't change. No single vendor can hold your capability hostage.

**Cost**
Intelligent routing matches each task to the cheapest model that clears your quality bar. Frontier models where they matter, small models where they don't. Clients cut token spend by up to 80%.

**Compound**
Not a loop that repeats — a helix that climbs. Control + Context + Choice + Cost closes the loop, then lifts it: every task your teams run returns the system to the same place, one turn higher. The compounding accrues to your firm.
